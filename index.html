Poona Panjarpole Trust

About Us

The Pune Panjarpole Trust, established in 1855, is a sanctuary for over 2,200 cattle. Located in Bhosari, the Trust provides shelter, food, and medical care to abandoned and aging cattle.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable and sacred environment for the welfare of cows and humanity.
